From https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipe/tomato-courgette-stew

Ingredients
1 tbsp olive oil

1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, crushed
3 courgettes, quartered lengthways and cut into chunks
2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
small bunch basil, torn
25g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), finely grated

Method

Heat the oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion
and cook for about 10 mins until softened and starting to go golden
brown. Add the garlic and cook for 5 mins more.

Add the courgettes and cook for about 5 mins until starting to
soften. Tip in the tomatoes and give everything a good stir. Simmer
for 35-40 mins or until tomatoes are reduced and courgettes soft, then
stir in the basil and Parmesan.
